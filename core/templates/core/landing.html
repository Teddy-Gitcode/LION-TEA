Lion Tea Backend

Empowering Farmers, Connecting Communities

Lion Tea is a digital platform designed to support tea farmers and stakeholders with modern tools for farm management, disease detection, weather insights, and more.

  • Farm Management: Track activities, harvests, and resources for better productivity.
  • Disease Detection: Use AI-powered tools to identify crop diseases early and take action.
  • Weather Monitoring: Get real-time weather updates tailored to your farm's location.
  • Drone Integration: Leverage drone technology for field analysis and monitoring.
  • E-commerce: Connect with buyers and sell your produce directly from the platform.
  • Community Chat: Engage with other farmers, share experiences, and get expert advice.

Our mission is to make farming smarter, more sustainable, and more profitable for everyone in the tea value chain.
